Guacamole

Description

This is a recipe on how to make good guacamole! I love guacamole ever since working at Chipotle and have made it numerous times for myself at home.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Avocados
  • 1/4 Cup Lime Juice
  • 1/4 Cup Onion
  • 1/8 Cup Jalapeno
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Cilantro

Steps

  1. Rinse off your avocados, lime, onion, jalapeno, and cilantro.
  2. Place your cilantro on cutting board and cut the cilantro into small, 1/8th pieces as best as possible. Be careful and do no not bruise the cilantro. Once done, set aside the cilantro.
  3. Rinse off cutting board and place jalapeno on it. Cut the jalapeno in half lengthwise, and then cut it in half lengthwise again. Then dice the jalapenos into 1/8th pieces. Don't make them big or you'll overpower the guac. Set aside once cut.
  4. Rinse off cutting board and place onion on it. Cut the onion in half and then start dicing the onion into 1/8th pieces. If they are too big they will overpower the guac. Set aside once cut.
  5. Rinse off the cutting board and place lime on it. Cut lime in half and set aside.
  6. Grab your avocados and one at a time, cut the avocado open and throwaway the pit. Once opened and pit is discarded, scoop the avocado into a mixing bowl or a bowl. Then, using a potato smasher, spatula, or spoon, mash the avocado until you a creamy texture. Or if you like it chunky, leave some chunks in it.
  7. Pour all of the prepared ingredients into the bowl along with the salt. Mix together evenly and bam! You have guac!
