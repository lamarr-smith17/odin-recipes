Jasmine Rice

Description

This is a recipe on how to make the PERFECT jasmine rice! It is one of my all-time favorite dishes to make. Let it be alone or paired with another dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Jasmine Rice
  • 1 1/4 Cups of Water

Steps

  1. Place rice in a bowl and fill with water. Swish with hand and drain, repeat this step 4 times.
  2. Put the cup of water and rice in a saucepan. Simmer on high heat, the surface should be rippling and the edges should be bubbling with white foam.
  3. Cover the rice with a lid and set it to low heat for 12 minutes. DO NOT LIFT LID!
  4. Let the rice stand for 10 minutes once its finished cooking.
  5. Fluff the rice using a spatula or rice paddle. Voila!
