Ramen

Description

A staple in college cuisine. Maruchan Ramen is infamous for helping students survive their tough semesters on a tight budget. With a little added ingredients, it can be very tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack Maruchan Chicken Ramen
  • Pepper
  • Hot sauce of your choosing
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place ramen into bowl as is or breakup the ramen. Then, fill the bowl with water until the noodles are submerged.
  2. Place in microwave for 3 minutes.
  3. Once done cooking, drain most of the water. Leave enough so that when you mix in the chicken powder, it sticks to the noodles and you have a little bit to sip on.
  4. Pour on the chicken powder and start mixing.
  5. Sprinkle some pepper on and hot sauce.
  6. There you go! You made every college students lifeline meal!
